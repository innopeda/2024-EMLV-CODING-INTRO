Why a cruise ?
  • A cruise allows you to visit several destinations without having to unpack and re-pack. You enjoy the comfort of the ship while exploring different cities.
  • From hiking in the mountains to visiting museums, including kayaking trips, there are many and varied options.
  • The cruise offers you the opportunity to taste typical dishes, from fresh seafood to regional specialties.
  • The atmosphere on board is often relaxing, with entertainment evenings and spaces to relax.
