Northern Europe
Cruising in Northern Europe is a unique and rewarding experience. Indeed, you will have the opportunity to admire beautiful landscapes, from Norwegian fjords to the Baltic islands, passing through incredible archipelagos. Each stop will allow you to discover different cultures, local traditions and fascinating history, whether in Stockholm, Copenhagen or Reykjavik. In addition, in summer, the temperatures are generally mild, which makes visits more pleasant.

Mediterranean
Cruising in the Mediterranean offers a unique experience for several reasons. First of all, the Mediterranean is known for its spectacular landscapes, with fine sandy beaches, turquoise waters and steep coasts. In addition, stops in historic cities such as Rome, Barcelona, Athens or Dubrovnik allow you to discover UNESCO World Heritage sites, museums and fascinating history. In addition, each region has its own cuisine. You can enjoy a variety of dishes, from Spanish tapas to Greek seafood. Finally, the Mediterranean enjoys a mild climate, especially in spring and autumn, making it an ideal destination almost all year round.
Come and discover and share friendly moments with your family!

Carribean
Cruising in the Caribbean can also be an unforgettable experience. Indeed, it will be the perfect opportunity to discover what they are famous for their white sandy beaches and crystal clear waters, perfect for swimming, coral reefs that shelter a rich marine life, Offering diving opportunities among colorful fish and turtles. On the other hand, each island has its own culture, a mix of African, European and indigenous influences. You can then explore local historical sites, museums and festivals. Taste typical dishes, from fresh seafood to local specialties like jerk chicken in Jamaica or grilled fish in Saint-Barthélemy. Most of the islands have a tropical climate, offering mild temperatures all year round, although the period from December to April is particularly popular.
In summary, a cruise to the Caribbean combines relaxation, adventure and cultural discoveries, all in a beautiful and sunny setting.
